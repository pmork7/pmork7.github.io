Every blog starts somewhere. This is mine.

## Why a blog?

I've been meaning to write more for a while. Not to build an audience or grow a following, but because writing forces me to think clearly. If I can't explain something in plain text, I probably don't understand it well enough.

## What I'll write about

Mostly the things I'm working on and learning: software architecture, tools I find interesting, problems I've solved (or failed to solve), and the occasional opinion on how we build software.

I'll aim for posts that are useful, honest, and short. No fluff.
